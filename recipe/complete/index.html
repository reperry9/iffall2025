Try these YUMMY Mini Chocolate Chip Pancakes from Allrecipes!

The best chocolate chip pancake recipe that is super easy to make for a yummy chocolaty breakfast treat! Chocolate chip pancakes are a varition of the traditional pancakes, where the chocolate chips are incorperated into the batter and create melted chocolate surprises within the fluffy pancake!

(73 Reviews)
30 minutes (10 min prep, 20 min cooking)
6 pancakes

Ingredients

    1 cup milk *
    1 large egg *
    1 cup all-purpose flour *
    2 tablespoons white sugar
    1 tablespoon baking powder
    1⁄4 teaspoon salt
    2 tablespoons butter (melted and cooled)
    1⁄2 cup mini chocolate chips
    * Allergens: dairy, egg, gluten

Materials

    Large skillet
    Bowl(s)
    Plate(s)
    Spatula

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Gather ingredients and materials listed above.
  2. Step 2 Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a separate bowl.
  3. Step 3 Whisk milk and egg together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.
  4. Step 4 Stir melted butter into the flour mixture, then whisk in milk mixture until smooth.
  5. Step 5 Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Step 6 Heat a small nonstick skillet over low heat. Pour about 1/3 cup batter into the hot skillet and cover with a lid. Cook pancake until browned on the bottom, about 3 minutes.
  7. Step 7 Flip pancake, then cover and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove to a plate and repeat to make remaining pancakes.
